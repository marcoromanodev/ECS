Our Service Area

Elite Clear Services is a family-owned business proudly serving Chicagoland for over 7 years, specializing in both commercial and residential cleaning. We provide top-tier service with attention to detail across a wide range of communities, including Oak Lawn, Cicero, the South Side of Chicago, and the South Loop.

Our service area covers key counties such as Cook, DuPage, Will, Grundy, Kane, and Kendall. In Cook County, we serve areas like Orland Park, La Grange, and Palos Heights, as well as the South Side and affluent suburbs like Western Springs. In DuPage County, we cater to cities like Naperville, Hinsdale, and Elmhurst. Our reach also extends to fast-growing suburbs in Will County, including Joliet and Plainfield.

We take pride in building strong relationships with these communities, offering trusted, thorough cleaning services to residential homes and commercial businesses throughout the region.
